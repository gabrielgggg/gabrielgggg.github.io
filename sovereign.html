Sovereign Default Reading List
Compiled by Gabriel Mihalache

This is a reading list I compiled in January 2020 for my second year graduate class, ECO 613 Computational Macroeconomics. The focus is on "quantitative-theoretic" models and relevant data work. Suggestions and edits are welcome! Back to my website.
