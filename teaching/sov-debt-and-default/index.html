Sovereign Default Reading List
Compiled by Gabriel Mihalache

This is a reading list I first compiled in January 2020, for my second year graduate class at Stony Brook University, ECO 613 Computational Macroeconomics. The focus is on "quantitative-theoretic" models and relevant data work. Suggestions and edits are welcome! Last updated August 2024. Back to my website.
